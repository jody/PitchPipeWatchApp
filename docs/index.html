PitchPipe App Framework for Apple Watch

Alpha 1.0.1

Usability:

  • Poor usability!
  • no instructions; non-obvious controls
  • Hints:
    • Area above Play button is scrollable to reveal additional controls
    • Clicking the multi-slider icon adds Frequency and Volume sliders below those controls

Functionality:

  • Plays sine-wave tone
  • Default tone is A4 based at 440Hz
  • Button selectable notes: C4, D4, E4, F4, G4, A4, B4, C5
  • Pitch frequency is modifiable by a slider
  • Volume is modifiable by a slider

Known Issues:

  • See Usability above
  • Tones have "cyclic clicking" (not always apparent at A4=440)
    • This likely reflects the loop-based tone construction not matching sampling length

Known Missing Functionality:

  • Allow chromatic (not just diatonic)
  • Greater range (not just C4 to C5)
